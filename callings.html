Adventurer

Fate

Sometimes Bara Bara sends you helpful visions to guide your paw. If something bad is about to happen, the Adventurer usu ally knows frst and might al ready be trying to avert it.

You may collect up to seven Mood Markers, but your Mood bonus can never exceed +3.

Risk: If something bad happens, you may throw a coin. One side means luck, the other doubles the consequences you have to face.

Paths

Wanderer's Path

Your stride is unbroken and there is so much to learn. You can collect up to seven Joy Markers.

Heart's Path

Your heart is capable of immense amounts of compassion. You can collect up to seven Love Markers.

Martyr's Path

You are very sensitive to pain, but you feel you must keep pushing onward. You can collect up to seven Greif Markers.

Shadow's Path

You are haunted by paranoid thoughts, but you'll be prepared once the moment comes. You can collect up to seven Fear Markers.

Claw's Path

Your soul burns with the fury of a thousand warriors. You can collect up to seven Anger Markers.

Artist

Fate

Your skill with the brush allows you to summon believable scenery. You can create illusions of all kinds, though they do not hold well against thorough inspection. They can serve as diversions, decorations or disguises. Tricking those that do not know what to look for with wet paint strokes.

Phantasms can be dispelled by a Detect roll. The Artist creates the illusion similar to using the Hide action, but may use whatever Emotion is most appropriate.

Leech Mood: Spend an Action and roll Emotion d6 against Emotion d6 of the victim. You steal one Mood Marker for every hit. You may set aside d6 to increase the number of stolen Mood Markers.

Give Mood: Spend an Action to distribute your Mood Markers among friends up to their limits.

Paths

The Sun

The sun entices those who live under its gaze. It brings joy and energy. Likewise the Artist takes Joy Markers from its enemies and shines them on its friends.

The Tree

The tree speaks of growth and life. It blossoms and nourishes those who live inside it. The Artist plucks Love Markers from its enemies, like leaves from a tree.

The Cloud

The cloud weeps at the horrible deeds it was forced to witness. The Artist takes Grief Markers from those that would rather hide behind them like a cloud.

The Moon

The moon only shows in the night, when most of the world is asleep. The Artist leeches Fear Markers away.

The Storm

The storm rages with reckless abandon. It devastates what has been thought safe. The Artist takes the Anger Markers away and sends them storming in another direction.

Bard

Fate

Your social pull is quite alluring. People tend to flock to you and heed your call. Charisma burns like a flame in the heart of the Bard. Others are easily swayed by your passion.

Perform: You spend an Action performing a song of the appropriate Emotion. Roll Emotion d6 to make allies within earshot gain 1 Mood Marker. Set aside d6 to make allies gain more Mood Markers.
