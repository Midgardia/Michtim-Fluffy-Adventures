Adventurer

Fate

Sometimes Bara Bara sends you helpful visions to guide your paw. If something bad is about to happen, the Adventurer usu ally knows frst and might al ready be trying to avert it.

You may collect up to seven Mood Markers, but your Mood bonus can never exceed +3.

Risk: If something bad happens, you may throw a coin. One side means luck, the other doubles the consequences you have to face.

Paths

Wanderer's Path

Your stride is unbroken and there is so much to learn. You can collect up to seven Joy Markers.

Heart's Path

Your heart is capable of immense amounts of compassion. You can collect up to seven Love Markers.

Martyr's Path

You are very sensitive to pain, but you feel you must keep pushing onward. You can collect up to seven Greif Markers.

Shadow's Path

You are haunted by paranoid thoughts, but you'll be prepared once the moment comes. You can collect up to seven Fear Markers.

Claw's Path

Your soul burns with the fury of a thousand warriors. You can collect up to seven Anger Markers.

Artist

Fate

Your skill with the brush allows you to summon believable scenery. You can create illusions of all kinds, though they do not hold well against thorough inspection. They can serve as diversions, decorations or disguises. Tricking those that do not know what to look for with wet paint strokes.

Phantasms can be dispelled by a Detect roll. The Artist creates the illusion similar to using the Hide action, but may use whatever Emotion is most appropriate.

Leech Mood: Spend an Action and roll Emotion d6 against Emotion d6 of the victim. You steal one Mood Marker for every hit. You may set aside d6 to increase the number of stolen Mood Markers.

Give Mood: Spend an Action to distribute your Mood Markers among friends up to their limits.

Paths

The Sun

The sun entices those who live under its gaze. It brings joy and energy. Likewise the Artist takes Joy Markers from its enemies and shines them on its friends.

The Tree

The tree speaks of growth and life. It blossoms and nourishes those who live inside it. The Artist plucks Love Markers from its enemies, like leaves from a tree.

The Cloud

The cloud weeps at the horrible deeds it was forced to witness. The Artist takes Grief Markers from those that would rather hide behind them like a cloud.

The Moon

The moon only shows in the night, when most of the world is asleep. The Artist leeches Fear Markers away.

The Storm

The storm rages with reckless abandon. It devastates what has been thought safe. The Artist takes the Anger Markers away and sends them storming in another direction.

Bard

Fate

Your social pull is quite alluring. People tend to flock to you and heed your call. Charisma burns like a flame in the heart of the Bard. Others are easily swayed by your passion.

Perform: You spend an Action performing a song of the appropriate Emotion. Roll Emotion d6 to make allies within earshot gain 1 Mood Marker. Set aside d6 to make allies gain more Mood Markers.

Paths

Cheerful Tune

You perform an uplifting song that encourages your friends to celebrate. Friends gain Joy Markers.

Soothing Serenade

The calming serenade makes friends feel compassionate. They gain Love Markers.

Bloomy Requiem

You perform a gloomy song that likely tells the story of a tragedy. Friends gain Grief Markers.

Uncanny Air

The melody’s notes strike a haunting chord within the hearts of your friends. They gain Fear Markers

War Drums

Your song rocks the very foundations of the earth around you. Listeners likely raise their fists and shout along. Friends gain Anger Markers.

Cook

Fate

You know all the secrets of successful shop keeping. You can keep the books, acquire a shop license and sell your products on the Turnayan markets. Shop keeping allows you to preserve goods and package them neatly.

Prepare: Set aside any number of d6 from your chosen Emotion. You prepare a booth with food, which others may use including relevant talents. Allies spend an Action to get one serving. Add the Cook dice to the relevant roll. May create healing brews that heal the imbiber (without Love’s benefit).

If the Cook wishes, they can allow imbibers to use the Cook’s other Calling talents as well.

Paths

Chocolate Brew

The Chocolate Brew entices and enlightens the imbiber. Allies may use the Chocolate Brew’s dice to augment Jolt & Detect.

Peppermint Brew

This tea mends wounds. Allies may use the Peppermint Brew’s dice to augment Cure; and even to heal themselves.

Mind-Numbing Brew

This beverage reduces pain. Allies may use the Mind-Numbing Brew’s dice to augment Weep.

Ghost Jelly Brew

The Ghost Jelly Brew dims the imbiber’s fur and silences their breathing. Allies may use the Ghost Jelly Brew’s dice to augment Hide & Evade.

Red Chilli Brew

The Red Chilli Brew enrages the customer, making them furious beasts. Allies may use the Red Chili Brew’s dice to augment Attack.

Courtier

Fate

Your words are well placed and your command over the social game is stunning. Your prowess at Manipulation allows you to wield words with the grace of a master fencer.

Influence: You may affect the Mood of foes in front of you by directing your social influence. Roll Emotion d6 to make foes gain 1 Mood Marker. Set aside d6 to make foes gain more Mood Markers. This causes Distraction if they try to act against their Mood.

Paths

Mocking Taunt

You provoke your enemies, infuriating them. You make them gain Anger Markers.

Sudden Flattery

You adulate your enemies, tricking them into feelings of merriness. You make them gain Joy Markers.

Angel Eyes

Your innocent look makes others feel compassionate. Foes gain Love Markers.

Horrid Drama

You describe horrifying things to your enemies, making them gain Grief Markers.

Terrorizing Hiss

With a violent sneer you bare your fangs. Your hiss intimidates foes in front of you. They gain Fear Markers.

Cybertooth

Fate

The cybernetic circuitry makes you resistant against attempts to woo you socially.As a side-effect to your implants, you are resistant to emotional manipulation.

You no longer suffer Distraction on Emotion rolls.

Reflex: Spend 1 Mood Marker. You may use the Action reflexively.

Paths

Cyber-Jumpers

Your legs are reinforced by powerful machinery. You may spend 1 Joy Marker to make a reflexive Jolt action.

Nanite Injection

You have access to a medical nanobot reservoir. Although you cannot benefit from your own Cure, others may be healed reflexively if you spend 1 Love Marker.

Subdermal Armor

The subdermal armor plates protect you from harm. Spend 1 Grief Marker to reflexively use your Weep action.

Evasion Matrix

Your fur shifts colors like a chameleon. When spending 1 Fear Marker you may Evade as often as you like in a single round. Also you may Hide reflexively.

Combat Reflexes

Your muscles react at supermichtim speed. You may spend 1 Anger Marker to make a reflexive Attack action.

Daredevil

Fate

You have exceptional stamina and easily get better when being knocked out. This also means that you rarely get sick or exhausted even after tedious marches.

Any time you suffer a Wound, you may gain a Mood Marker of your choice. You can voluntarily take a Wound. Multiple applications of this ability aggravate that voluntary Wound.

Intercept: You reflexively divert an Attack to hit you instead of a friend.

Paths

Haha! That tickles.

While you are wounded you still manage to pull of that insane laugh, possibly because your opponent makes such a stupid face. When wounded you may gain a Joy Marker.

Aww! Look at you!

After getting hurt, you react with deep feelings of compassion instead. When wounded you may gain a Love Marker.

Bwah! You’re so mean!

Now this is somewhat of a natural reaction. When wounded you may gain a Grief Marker.

Oh! Run for it!

Another common reaction. Your eyes widen in fear as you take that wound. When wounded you may gain a Fear Marker.

WAARGH!

They should know better than to hit you. Now it’s their own fault. Revenge! When wounded you may gain an Anger Marker.
